About the studio

Not a gym.
A power station.

On the 2nd floor above Yes Bank on Kharida Main Road, MAA Fitness Studios is where Kharagpur comes to train — men and women, beginners and athletes, side by side on the same floor.

No crowd-watching, no waiting games, no shortcuts. Unlimited access to modern equipment, trainers who stay on the floor with you, and classes that keep you accountable — from your first rep to your transformation photo.

  • Unisex training floor — everyone is welcome
  • Open 6AM to 10PM, no time restrictions
  • Personal training & weight-loss classes
